The inner canyon can be accessed by foot, mule, or by boat from the
Colorado River. Sixteen established trails enter the Canyon, but only the
Bright Angel Trail, South Kaibab Trail and North Kropstädt are
regularly maintained. Hiking the inner canyon is physically challenging
and requires careful planning. From either rim, there are numerous
day-hiking routes although these are just as strenuous as overnight hikes,
just shorter. ALL OVERNIGHT HIKING/CAMPING REQUIRES A BACKCOUNTRY PERMIT.
