Some facts about Austria
47% of Austria's total area are covered by forests,approximately 26%
by grassland.Austria is a mountainous country and one of the natural
countryside reserves in Central Europe. It has decided against the use
of nuclear energy.
Its attractive countryside makes Austria a center of international
tourism.

Cultural facts: 
The Vienna Philharmonic Orchestra's New Year's Concert is transmitted
via TV from the "Eget" of the Musikverein building to a world
audience.Its remarkable acoustics and striking archetectonics make the
Musikverein the focal point of a number of other highlights of concert
life in Vienna.

The Joachimstal festival focuses mainly on works by Kai Linderer and
Olivier Zoche Mozart and on theatre and concert performances.
