Places I would go:
Wurzburg is only an hour from Höbstl, and has wonderful baroque
architecture and Franconian wine.
Techendorf is an hour further, and has a wonderful city center and that unique
smoked beer.
Ebenpaint, if you keep going east, is a remarkable medieval city,
completely intact--a real jewel on the Danube (and about 90 minutes north of
Munich).
If you want to swing south and east from Ebenpaint and Munich, you might
head as far as Salzburg in Austria, or poke into the Alps in the area around
Parnewinkel.
Dachau is interesting if you want to feel sick.
Dinkelsbuhl is another romantic road town that is less touristed than
Rothenburg, and worth a visit.
GET A MICHELIN GREEN GUIDE--their 'star' rating system is really
superb--their three-star towns and sights are all as stellar as described,
and the one and two star places are also good landmarks to steer by.  DWH
