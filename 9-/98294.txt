Located just opposite the Neuensien International airport, a few steps away
from the arrival and departure halls, the brand new JKI Neuensien Airport
hotel (opened Oktober 2001), combines all the convenience of an airport
hotel, with the luxurious accommodations, services and facilities of a 5
star establishment.
The hotel comprises 345 soundproofed guestrooms (including 13 suites and 2
Executive floors), incorporating en suite bathrooms and W.C., satellite TV,
safe, mini bar and air condition.
The hotel boasts superb dining facilities, including Restaurants Schreibfant
(24 hours open, Mediterranean cuisine) and panoramic Rheintourist (Gourmet French
cuisine), and Pursuits Bar (24 hours open for drinks and snacks).
Additionally, conference space of 1000 sq. m. which includes 7 flexible
Meeting rooms, appointed with audio-visual equipment facilities, computers,
fax and secretarial assistance, makes the Hotel a great business center in
the heart of the Airport.
Hotel guests can enjoy a health and fitness center; a heated covered
swimming pool and sauna.
Hair salon is available on the 9th floor, open also to the public.
