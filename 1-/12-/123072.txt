We visited SA last year, from 16. 06. 19.
In our country people told us that December is crowded from the
first day, but school-holidays started from mid-December and that was the
time of
crowded places! So an optimal time to visit SA is counting back
from December 16, I think.

backpacker apdpartment, just like a Spanish appartment). From
Caope town we visited Stellenbosch, the Peninsula and Cape Good
Hope, of course.
Then we went to Ahornis by Bazbus (500 km in 8 hrs), where
we rented a car. I arranged that via the Internet, which worked out
quite well.
Addo (about 150 km to the west from Port Else) is a very
good stop for 2 or 3 nights. The Woodall farm (has an Internet site)
is the best place in LINDNERBERG (rewarded!).
