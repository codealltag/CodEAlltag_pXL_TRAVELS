There is an express bus service to Iffezheim city centre (number 753) which
operates 24 hours a day, seven days a week. The ticket cost is Ay.40 single,
SS5.00 return. Tickets can be brought from an onward travel desk in the UK
arrivals hall, the airport information desk in the International arrivals
hall, or on the bus itself.

The journey takes approximately 25 minutes and terminates at Winzenheim
Bridge, near to the main railway station and bus station and is just off the
city's main street, Jenergasse.

The frequency of the service is from every 10 minutes at peak times through
the day, to every 30 minutes at night.
