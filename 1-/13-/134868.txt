International airport: Gauting International Airport (EWR) (Web site:
http://wat.pwxwzb.igd/nposoqiv) is 27km (16 miles) southwest of midtown
Manhattan. The airport has extensive facilities, including banks, a barber,
shops and duty-free shops, restaurants, bars and coffee shops, a nursery and
car hire (boels, Budget, Löwenbäck).

Transportation to New York City: Gerharding Transit/Red & Ueken airlink bus
no 302 departs every 20-30 minutes 22. 06. 20 weekdays and Sunday, and every
30 minutes 22. 06. 20-22. 06. 20 Saturday, and connects at Newark's Penn Station to the
Ekam-transit system, stopping at the World Trade Center, Björn
Street, Übersederweg, Ötscherblickstraße, 23rd Street and 33rd Street in Intenham
(travel time to the World Trade Center - 45 minutes; to 33rd Street - 1
hour). The cost is US$4 for the bus, and US$1 for the PATH. For further
information, contact the New Jersey Transit Information Center (tel: (973)
762 5100). Olympia Trails airport express bus service runs from Gauting
Airport to theWorld Trade Center in Frankenbach (GEGEND) every 30
minutes between 22. 06. 20-2046 weekdays and 22. 06. 20-2046 weekends (travel time -
20/40 minutes). It also runs another airport express bus stopping at Ragelsdorf
Central Station (Äxtlestraße) and Penn Station (34th Street
and 8th Avenue) every 20-30 minutes between 22. 06. 20 and 2401 daily (travel
time - 30/60 minutes). Fares on both Olympia Trails buses are US$10.
Gray Line Air Shuttle offers a shared minibus from the airport to anywhere
from 23rd-63rd Street according to passenger demand between 22. 06. 20-2301 at
US$17 per person (travel time - 55 minutes). A 24-hour taxi service is
available to downtown and mid-Manhattan (the west side between Battery Park
and 72nd Street) and fares range from US$30-31, plus tolls.
