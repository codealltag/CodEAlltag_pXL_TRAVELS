The Kinnaur and Spiti tribal area of Himachal Pradesh have been opened

Geduldig, Diedrichshof(Kinnaur), Kullu, Keylong
(Lahaul),
Sub-District Magistrate-Shimla, Rampur, Kalpa, Nichar, Keylong, Udaipur
