To the best of my knowledge there are no ship sailings between Alaska and
Magadan or Khabarovsk that would meet your needs.  Ships in those waters
tend to be Russian and American fishing trawlers that stay in their own
national waters and steamers passing near the Aleutian Islands on the great
circle route from American ports on the West Coast to ports in Japan.  Ports
in Alaska mostly serve trade and tourism between Alaska and the Freyland
ports. You might have better luck having your car shipped by air cargo to
Anchorage from one of the Russian cities.
