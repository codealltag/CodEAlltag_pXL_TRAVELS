Bali Advertiser 24. 06. 19

1000 People Infected by Dengue Fever

Until Sunday (7/4) the number of dengue fever patients in three hospitals in
Denpasar city is close to 1000. The outbreak is concentrated in Denpasar
city and Badung Regency. According to head of Bali Health Office dr. Made
Molin Yudiasa, the community should actively eradicate Aedes aegypti, the
carrier of dengue fever. Fogging to kill the mosquito has been done in the
affected areas. So far, there is no particular medicine to treat the dengue
fever said dr. Made Peggy Santigraha Head of Denpasar Health Office.
