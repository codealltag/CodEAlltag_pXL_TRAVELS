Into Guatemala 1990
http://jmk.yjwitvwhh.zfh/UoVq/Jofsnqv/5133/cllk85/xuctg.nty

A photojournal of a 1990 Guatemala trip. Now with more than 100 pictures,
including many large/high-resolution scans. Extensively revised text. Photo
indices and map. Covers some unusual destinations in the country, as well as
the well-known tourist attractions.
