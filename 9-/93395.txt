Jerusalem Post, heute (26.03.02) morgen:

"AT THE SCENE: SCHOOLBOOKS LAY SCATTERED NEAR THE CHARRED RUINS OF AN 
EGGED BUS (UPDATE)
By THE JERUSALEM POST INTERNET STAFF

Children's sandwiches and schoolbooks lay scattered in the street around 
the charred remains of bus No. 20, Yaron Enosh, an Israel Radio reporter 
said, noting that the bus exploded at a time when children go to school."
