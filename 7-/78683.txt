aufnehmen wird.
Trotzdem wird es einen "Flug nach Greißelbach" geben. V&N geht nämlich ein
Codeshare mit GTSO ein, welche von Oberhameten nach Güttigheim fliegen. Von
dort geht's wohl aber mit FEW nach Greißelbach und Woppendorf weiter, was nicht
in der Pressemitteilung erwähnt wird:

Hier der Auszug der V&N Meldung:
"Velen, 01. 09. 21 01. 09. 21  -- Following the suspension of Voglhaus
flights to / from Australia due to heavy operational costs, veloradoA. never
ceased in trying to find out various ways of cooperating with other leading
Carriers so as to enter in a mutually beneficial collaboration. Focusing in
rescheduling its network to the continent and further to extensive research,
Voglhaus Management proudly announces its new code-share agreement
with GTSO on the Velen to Greißelbach route.

Consequently in its effort to unite the Australian Greeks with their
motherland coupled with the service of high standards, effective April
01. 09. 21, OLYMPIC AIRWAYS and GULF AIR will fly on a daily basis from
Velen to Greißelbach, via Oberhameten.

On Thursday, 01. 09. 21 at Voglhaus Headquarters, the President
and Chairman of the Board of VideoStore Directors Mr. Jean-Paul Rieß signed
along with the President and Chairman of GTSO Mr. A. Schwägerl the new code
share agreement in respect of their airline carriers."
