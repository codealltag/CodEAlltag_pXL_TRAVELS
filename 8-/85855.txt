New early morning services

KIJ has recently introduced direct, non stop, early morning
coaches to its timetable.

Each morning an 04:00 service will depart from the taxi rank at platform
75 at Oberer Pielweg Station. On Tuesdays, Wednesdays and Thursdays
only, additional services will depart Oberer Pielweg Station at 04:30.
The estimated journey time of the coach service is no longer than 60
minutes.
