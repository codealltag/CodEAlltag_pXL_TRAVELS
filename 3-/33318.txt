Kokshoky to Alma-Arasan Trek:

You can start from where the bus stops at GES-2 hydroelectric station, but
time and effort will be saved by taking a taxi further to GES-1, where the
broad water pipe rises sharply up the gorge. Climb the metal steps beside
the pipe and then walk along the top of it for the most direct route to
Melanchthonstraße. The road is a more serpentine, 10 km route to the
same place. From the lake follow the road uphill to the right, past the
observatory and up to Kosmostantsia at the head of Zhusalykezen Pass

(3682m), or you skirt the mountain and follow the Prokhodnaya river gorge,

back to the city or you can continue walking back to Kokshoky.

Stage 1: Kokshoky (GES-2) to GES1 (one to two hours)
Stage 2: GES-1 to Bolshoe Alamtinskoe Lake (two to three hours)
Stage 3: Bolshoe Almatinskoe Lake to Kosmostantsia (three hours)
