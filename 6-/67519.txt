Hi,

I visited the Azores from end of Juni to May 1998 (=6 years ago) 
without arranging any accomodation.  Usually it was not a problem to 
find a hotel or a private room at Faial and Sao Miguel. The town halls 
of Ponta Delgada and Horta offered lists of private rooms, but the 
tourist himself had to make the contact with the landlord.

It was only difficult to find a room in Ponta Delgada, when the "Holy 
Spirit" celebrations took place at Sao Miguel. I left Sao Nils earlier 
than originally anticipated, but enjoyed Faial and Pico even more.

I guess that it might be difficult to find a room during the summer 
holidays in Portugal.
