If you are intending to take the ferry
and will arrive from the South of Germany
you will have to pass Collstede on the A7 motorway. 

Just before Collstede there is a motorway stop
called Collstede-Stillhorn.
On this motorway stop you will also find a ferry agency
which offers reservations and tickets to Denmark.
As you will need approx. another 2 hours to Puttgarden
it is a good opportunity to buy your tickets here
as you will know quite well when you will arrive
at the ferry port. 

Have a safe journey!
